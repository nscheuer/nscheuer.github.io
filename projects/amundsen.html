Ongoing project to practice and learn modern robotics concepts and applying these.

Software

Developing a live robotics dashboard for visualizing the positions, sensor readings, and commands of multiple robots in real time.

  • Backend stack in Go, ingesting live ROS2 telemetry and storing data in PostgreSQL and Redis.
  • Frontend visualization with CesiumJS world map; Zustand as datastore; efficient position history via WebSockets.
  • Backend supports diverse telemetry: position, IMU, encoders, images, and LiDAR data.
  • Containerized with Docker and deployed via Kubernetes for scalable, production-grade operation.

Hardware

Designing and building a robot from scratch, including custom electronics and mechanical integration.

  • PCB design in KiCAD; evaluation of electrical components (MOSFETs, wiring, fuses) for expected loads.
  • Soldering and assembly of a battery management system with LiPo batteries, including reverse-polarity protection.
  • Integration of Nvidia Jetson Nano with CAN bus to drive ST B-G431B-ESC1 motor controllers.
  • Encoder programming and oscilloscope validation of quadrature signals.
  • Configuration and tuning of ST motor driver boards.

Future Work

  • Mechanical design and 3D-printing parts for integration of motors and treads.
  • Communication with robot using LoRA and FPV video downlink.
