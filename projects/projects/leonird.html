LEONIRD is a CubeSat mission that employs a Fourier Transform Near-Infrared (FT-NIR) spectrometer to measure trace gases using solar occultation. I support the team through the Small Satellite Collaborative, with contributions including:

  • Mission planning support and attitude determination and control system (ADCS) analysis
  • Development, integration, and sharing of ADCS-related software and tools
  • Assistance with broader ADCS tasks across mission design and operations
