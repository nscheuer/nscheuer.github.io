This paper formally derives two stability regions for ring system of N vehicles, and formally proves stabilizability using only one controlled vehicle. The results are reinforced by simulation results. An overview:

  • Defining convoy dynamics as a feedback interconnection of subsystems
  • Deriving stability regions using the definitions of strong frequency domain stability (SFSS) and interconnection stability
  • Detailled derivation of stabilizability using properties of block Toeplitz matrices
  • Stability regions and LQR control validated using simulation
