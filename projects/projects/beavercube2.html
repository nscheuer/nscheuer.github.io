BeaverCube 2 is an oceanographic research cubesat developed by MIT STARLab and MIT Portugal. It aims to use onboard image inference to enable more efficient data collection and reduce downlink requirements. I worked on specifically:

  • Modelling and validation of control/estimator schemes
  • Flight computer architecture and mission operations (CONOPS)
  • Supported flatsat integration and testing workflows
  • Technical coordination and project organization, including hiring and onboarding of new team members
  • Establishing and maintaining GitHub workflows, documentation standards, and cross-team development practices
