Between 2023 and 2025, I was part of the teaching team for the Control Systems sequence at ETH Zurich’s Institute for Dynamic Systems and Control (IDSC), progressing from Teaching Assistant to Lab Supervisor and ultimately Head Teaching Assistant for Control Systems II.

  • Head Teaching Assistant (Control Systems II) — led a team of 9 TAs supporting over 300 students; coordinated recitations, course administration, and weekly teaching activities.
  • Designed and graded programming challenges and control demonstrations; contributed to exam design, invigilation, and evaluation.
  • Lab Supervisor — supervised weekly control laboratory sessions, maintained and upgraded lab equipment and software, and expanded hands-on teaching modules; mentored 100+ students.
  • Teaching Assistant (Control Systems I & II) — taught recitations for groups of 30+ students and developed interactive programming challenges as part of ETH’s Innovendum Project.
