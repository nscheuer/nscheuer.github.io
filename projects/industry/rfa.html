I spent the latter half of 2024 working at Rocket Factory Augsburg, hopping between the GNC (Guidance, Navigation, and Control), OSD (Operational Software Development), Launch, and Aerodynamics Teams. It was a very impactful experience, and I worked on a plethora of different projects. Some highlights:

  • Creating a day of launch dashboard, merging live datastreams to determine launch windows
  • Automation of Monte Carlo simulation pipelines, including failure injection studies
  • Writing post-processing scripts for HIL (hardware-in-the-loop) tests.
  • Built, networked, and maintained high-altitude weather balloon receiver stations; integrated data into the automated launch availability pipeline
